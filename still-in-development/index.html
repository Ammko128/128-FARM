128-FARM

Chaotic co-op farming • Rogue-lite seasons • 1-4 players

Engine: Unity Platform: PC / Xbox Status: Prototype
Team up. Plant. Panic. 128-FARM throws cooperative farming into pure chaos — randomized seasons, frantic events, and emergent disasters where every run becomes a frantic story you and your friends will laugh about for weeks.

Procedural Biomes

Six major biomes with seasonal variants. Each run mixes layout, hazards, and events to keep the team guessing.

Chaotic Co‑op

Local & online 1–4 player support. Roles are emergent — coordinate or watch your plan collapse gloriously.

Rogue‑lite Progression

Unlock permanent upgrades between seasons: faster tools, resilient seeds, and wild mutators.

Eventful Gameplay

From crows to eruptions, random events force players to improvise — and create memorable run stories.
